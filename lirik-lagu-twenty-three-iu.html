¤ Cardinal Numbers.

Cardinal Numbers adalah angka yang menentukan berapa banyak dari sesuatu yang ada.

=> Example :

▪︎17 = seventeen.

▪︎231 = two hundred and thirty one.

▪︎1.025 = one thousand twenty five.

▪︎Andi mempunyai 25 kelereng = andi has 25 (twenty five) marbles.

=> Penyelesaian Soal :

123 = One hundred and twenty three (E).

16. Eighty three times twenty five minus fifty three plus twenty five minus ten.What is the result?​


Eighty three = 83

Times = kali(×)

twenty five = 25

minus = minus(-)

fifty three = 53

plus = tambah(+)

twenty five = 25

minus = minus (-)

ten = 10

83×25-53+25-10 = 2037 (two thousand thirty seven)
