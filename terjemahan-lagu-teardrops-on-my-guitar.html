9. 45. "my father does not play guitar" change this sentence into interrogative sentence! a. my father plays guitarb. does my father plays guitar?c. does my father play guitar?d. does my father playing guitar?​


Jawaban:

B. Does my father play guitar?

PEMBAHASAN

my father does not play guitar" change this sentence into interrogative sentence!

=> Perintah soal adalah mengubahnya menjadi kalimat tanya.

Sebelum mengubahnya mari kita bahas Present Tense,

Yaitu sebuah tenses untuk menyatakan Kegiatan berulang atau kebenaran umum / FAKTA.

Rumus Present Tense(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O?

kalimat Present Tense

(+) my father plays guitar

(-) my father does not play guitar

(?) Does my father play guitar? ✔️

Catatan untuk Subjek “My Father”(+) Pada kalimat Positive VERB+s (drinks)(-) dan (?) Tapi pada kalimat Negative dan Interrogative kembali VERB 1 tanpa (-s/es)

15. my mum + sing /- play the guitar ​


+ = positive

- = negative

Jawaban:

my mom can sing

my mom cant play the guitar

my mom can sing but she can't play the guitar.

It seems like you're referring to your mom's musical abilities. If you're saying "my mum can sing," it means your mom has the talent or ability to sing. If you're saying "my mum can play the guitar," it means your mom has the skill to play the guitar. Both singing and playing the guitar are musical talents that can be enjoyed and appreciated.
