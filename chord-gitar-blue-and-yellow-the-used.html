14. a box contains green and yellow balls 7/13 of the balls are green and 6/13 are blue what percentage of the balls are yellow(please help me)​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Sure, I'd be happy to help!

You mentioned that 7/13 of the balls are green and 6/13 are blue. To find out the percentage of balls that are yellow, you can subtract the sum of the percentages of green and blue balls from 100%.

Percentage of green balls = (7/13) * 100% = 53.85%

Percentage of blue balls = (6/13) * 100% = 46.15%

Now, to find the percentage of yellow balls:

Percentage of yellow balls = 100% - (Percentage of green balls + Percentage of blue balls)

Percentage of yellow balls = 100% - (53.85% + 46.15%) = 100% - 100% = 0%

Therefore, the percentage of yellow balls is 0%

Pembahasan

Chord atau nama purenya adalah akor yaitu sekumpulan nada-nada yang digabungkan secara bersama sehingga menimbulkan bunyi yang harmonis. Dalam alat musik gitar, ada beberapa kunci dasar dalam akord, diantaranya :

Kunci C, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 2 senar (1st) , 4 senar (2rd), dan 5 senar (3th) (fret)Kunci D, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 3 senar (1st), 1 senar (2rd), dan 2 senar (3th) (fret)Kunci E, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 3 senar (1st), 5 (2rd), 4 (3th).

Ada juga kunci-kunci lain biasa digunakan yaitu :

Kunci F.Kunci G.Kunci A.Kunci Dm <D minor>.Kunci Em <E minor>.Kunci Am <A minor>.

23. The ratio of the length of blue ribbon to the length of yellow ribbon is 7:9. The sum of the length of blue ribbon and the length of yellow ribbon is 272 cm. The difference in its length is .... ​


34cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

= 7 + 9

= 16

= (272 : 16)

= 17

Blue ribbon = 17 × 7

= 119 cm

Yellow ribbon = 17 × 9

= 153 cm

Difference in lenght = 153cm - 119cm = 34cm
